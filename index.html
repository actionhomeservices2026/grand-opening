A note from the founder

For more than a decade we've designed and built outdoor spaces across the Greater Toronto Area. On June 6, 2026, we open the doors to something we've been working toward for years — the largest outdoor landscape design centre in North America.

It was built for the design and architecture community as much as it was built for our clients. A place to walk through fully realized environments — lighting, water, structure, planting, materials — and have informed conversations before a project ever begins.

We'd be honoured to have you with us for the opening.

John Karpov
Founder, Action Home Services
